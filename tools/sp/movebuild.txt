Moves langusage specific tree under D:\svcpack.x86chk to release or a release tree back to langauge directory under D:\svcpack.x86chk

movebuild [-n:build# | -qfe: qfe#] [-d:direction]

-n:[buildnum] build number to move.
-qfe:[qfe number]
-d:[direction] either forward to release or back to binaries.
