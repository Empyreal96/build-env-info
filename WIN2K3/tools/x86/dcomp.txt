DCOMP -llog [-x .ext ...] [-m#] [-d] [-bbom] Source Dest

  -x .ext        Exclude files with extension .ext.
  -m             Force use of # threads.
  -v             Verbose.
  -llog          Log filename, dcomp.log default.
  -binfPath      your setup\inf\win4\inf path.
  -f infname     Excludes files listed in inf.
  Source         Source file specification.  Wildcards may be used.
  Destination    Specifies directory where renamed compressed files
                 will be placed.
