Usage: HssSetupTool <options> <command> <parameters>

Available commands:

  BINPLACE <sku file> <setup image file> <root directory> <object directory>
  COMPILE  <root directory> <sku>
  LIST     <input cabinet>
  EXTRACT  <input cabinet> <file>
  INSTALL  <input cabinet>

  UNPACK   <input cabinet> <directory>
  PACK     <directory> <output cabinet>
